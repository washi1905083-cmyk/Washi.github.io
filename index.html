01About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Mechanical Engineering graduate from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), where I completed my thesis on the aerodynamic impact of tall buildings using Computational Fluid Dynamics.

My work bridges classical engineering simulation and modern AI systems. At Scale AI, I applied domain expertise to evaluate LLM outputs — identifying hallucinations and reasoning failures, and providing structured RLHF feedback integrated into training pipelines.

I believe rigorous engineering thinking — forged through fluid dynamics, thermodynamics, and numerical analysis — translates powerfully into evaluating and improving intelligent systems.
